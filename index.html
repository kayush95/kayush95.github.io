I am a senior undergraduate at the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur , enrolled in a bachelor's program in Computer Science and Engineering, scheduled to graduate in 2017.

My research studies deep learning and its applications to computer vision. I was a research intern (Summer 2016) at the Big Data Experience Lab at Adobe Research Bangalore, working with Gaurush Hiranandani on Augmented Reality for Enterprise. Prior to this (Summer 2015), I was a visiting student researcher at the Video Analytics Lab at the Indian Institute of Science Bangalore, working with Srinivas S. S. Kruthiventi, supervised by Prof. R. Venkatesh Babu, on Saliency Prediction.

At my home college, I have been fortunate to work with a variety of faculty on interesting projects.
