About Me

I am a final year undergraduate pursuing B.Tech (Hons.) in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur. My research studies computer vision and machine learning. Scene understanding models excel with large amounts of labeled data, however this is expensive to scale.
