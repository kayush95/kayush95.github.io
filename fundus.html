Segmentation of Retinal Vessels in Fundus Images using Generative Adversarial Networks.

  • Guide: Prof. Pabitra Mitra
  • A part of my undergraduate thesis also focuses on designing class conditional GAN architectures for biomedical image processing, specifically segmentation of blood vessels in fundus images. GANs have been used in the domain of natural images but its applicability in medical imaging domain is yet to be explored. So, I have also taken up the challenging task of semi supervised learning by GANs on fundus images.
